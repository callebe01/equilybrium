# Equilybrium Landing Page Project

## About the Brand

Equilybrium is a health technology company focused on burnout prevention through AI and machine learning. Their tagline is "For your mind, for your life." The platform analyzes work patterns, sleep data, and health metrics to provide personalized insights and early warnings about potential burnout risks.

Website: https://equilybrium.health/

## Value Proposition

Equilybrium helps individuals and teams prevent burnout before it happens. The platform uses AI powered wellness monitoring to keep users balanced, productive, and healthy. It serves both self employed professionals and team leaders.

### Core Pillars

1. **Prevention**: Detects early signs of burnout weeks before they become serious (3 to 4 weeks ahead of traditional methods)
2. **Science Backed**: Built on clinical research and validated metrics
3. **Privacy First**: End to end encryption and zero knowledge architecture. Data is never sold, shared, or misused

## Key Features

- **Wellness Score**: Daily score showing overall mental wellness based on activity patterns
- **AI Insights**: Personalized recommendations powered by machine learning
- **Data Privacy**: End to end encryption and zero knowledge architecture
- **Trend Analysis**: Track progress over time and identify patterns affecting wellness
- **Smart Alerts**: Notifications when patterns suggest a break or attention is needed
- **Actionable Tips**: Science based recommendations tailored to specific situations

## Target Audience

- **Individuals**: Professionals who want to monitor and manage their own mental wellness
- **Businesses**: Teams and organizations looking to support employee wellbeing and productivity

## Benefits

1. **Early Detection**: Identify burnout signs weeks before they become serious problems
2. **Career Protection**: Stay productive and avoid career setbacks that come with burnout
3. **Mental Wellness**: Improve overall mental health with data driven insights
4. **Get Time Back**: Optimize work patterns for efficiency and more free time
5. **Peak Productivity**: Find optimal work rhythm and maintain sustainable high performance
6. **Data Control**: Users own their data with full export, delete, or modify capabilities

## Brand Identity

- **Colors**: Dark navy/teal as primary, with soft mint/teal accents. Light gray backgrounds. The overall palette conveys trust, calm, and professionalism
- **Typography**: Clean, modern sans serif fonts
- **Logo**: A circular emblem with organic/leaf like elements paired with the "equilybrium" wordmark and the subtitle "For your mind, for your life"
- **Tone**: Professional, caring, empowering. Emphasis on control and prevention

## Current Website Structure

Single page layout with the following sections:
1. Hero (tagline, CTA buttons: Start Free / Request Demo)
2. Features (6 feature cards)
3. Benefits (6 benefit cards)
4. Integrations (browsers and wearables tabs)
5. Compliance (privacy certifications)
6. Pricing (individual and business tabs)
7. FAQ (categorized: Getting Started, Account & Billing, Privacy & Security, Features & Usage, Technical Support)
8. CTA section (For Individuals / For Businesses)
9. Footer (Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, Support)

## Tech Stack

The current Equilybrium website is built with **React** (client side rendered SPA) and **Tailwind CSS** for styling. Our landing page must follow the same stack:

- **React** as the UI framework
- **Tailwind CSS** for utility first styling
- Project bootstrapped with a standard React toolchain (e.g. Vite + React)
